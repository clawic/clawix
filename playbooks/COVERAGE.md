# Coverage Matrix

Coverage states:

- `covered`: a ready or reference playbook exists with critical cases and checklist evidence.
- `partial`: an index, placeholder, or broad playbook exists, but capability-specific coverage is incomplete.
- `missing`: no useful playbook exists yet.
